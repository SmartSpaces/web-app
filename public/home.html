How it Works

1. Personality Insights

Everyone at the co-working space takes the test and an anonymous result emerges per company.

2. Optimize Co-Working

Empowered with the data, co-working spaces can then optimize their offices based on SmartSpace Insights.

3. Goes both ways

Co-Working Space Managers are empowered but startups are too. They can see everyone's score and choose the best space for them.
